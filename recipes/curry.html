Brown broth with depths of red homogenous with the broth, there lays subtle islands of chicken and a drumstick piece propped above the surface of the curry acting like a garnish

Elements needed

  • Steel
  • Lead poisoning
  • Jack daniels Old number 7
  • 3 Blue encubated chicken eggs

Instructions to assemble curry

  1. Melt steel at 2,300 fahrenheit for 2minutes and cool using 200ml of water then pour into a mold of a curry bowl. Preheat oven to broil
  2. Buy extracted blood with lead poisoning on the black market and season with salt and pepper, heat until boiling then transfer to the mold and put in the oven for 20minutes
  3. As the oven cooks our blood raise 3 blue chickens and wait for adult chicken to excrete. Harvest blue jam and mix with 2 cups of jack daniels old number 7 in a mixer with ice. Filter out particles of jam with strainer into a cup.
  4. Now when the blood is finished baking, take it out and pour the mixture of blue daniels into the blood and watch your soup transform into a nice chicken curry!
